## The Big Idea

The Divine Blueprint tries to explain reality as a bounded, self-checking,
self-updating information pattern.

That sounds big because it is big. It is a theory-of-everything-scale candidate
framework.

But it is not proven yet.

## Why A New Framework Is Being Suggested

Modern physics is powerful, but incomplete.

It has excellent models for particles and forces. It has excellent models for
gravity. But those models do not fully merge. Physics also still has deep
questions about dark matter, dark energy, measurement, and why patterns repeat
across scale.

The Divine Blueprint asks:

```text
What if these are different views of one deeper pattern?
```

## The Five Big Ideas

1. Scale repetition: patterns can look related when you zoom in or out.
2. Boundary limits: finite regions cannot hold infinite information.
3. Recursive flow: force-like quantities can change across energy levels by a
   controlled rule.
4. Fixed point: a stable reality must be self-consistent.
5. Generative succession: a pattern can produce a new related pattern.

## Why W[F] -> F' Is Important

The symbol:

```text
W[F] -> F'
```

means a process `W` takes a pattern `F` and produces a successor `F'`.

The successor is:

- similar to the original
- not an exact clone
- bounded so it does not blow up

That is the math version of "copy with a controlled twist."
